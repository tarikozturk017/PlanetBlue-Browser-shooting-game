How to play the game?


Move the Spaceship


You can rotate your ship by pressing Left Arrow Key or Right Arrow Key

and thrust your ship by pressing Up Arrow

You can shoot the meteors by pressing Space Bar



Game Features




When the game is started, your ship is going to blink for a few seconds. During this time you cannot crash any meteor and lose your one of your lives

After a few seconds, meteors start to appear on the screen from different point of the edges.



Your goal is to shoot the meteors by pressing space bar on your keyboard

If you shoot them successfully, your score will go up. And if you kill all of the meteors on the screen, you will level up



If a meteor hit the PlanetBlue before you shoot them, PlanetBlue lose some health, and your score goes down

If you crash a meteor, your ship blows up and, you lose one of your lives and some scores

After your ship blows up, you restart to the game and your ship blinks again.

Every two level, a red ship icon with a circle around will appear on the secreen, randomly.

If you pass over that icon, you will earn scores, and you will earn one more life.

If you go throught the edges, you will come from the other side of the screen, and you lose some scores

When you lose all of your lives or PlanetBlue's health drops to 0, the game is over.
